Step-by-Step Admission Process

Follow these steps to successfully apply for admission:

  1. Step 1: Review the eligibility criteria for your chosen program on the Eligibility Criteria page.
  2. Step 2: Fill out the online application form available on the Admissions page.
  3. Step 3: Upload all required documents, including transcripts, certificates, and proof of identity.
  4. Step 4: Pay the application fee through the secure online payment portal.
  5. Step 5: Submit your application and note down your application ID for future reference.
  6. Step 6: Await the confirmation email with further instructions regarding entrance exams or interviews (if applicable).
  7. Step 7: Attend the entrance exam or interview as scheduled.
  8. Step 8: Receive your admission decision via email.
  9. Step 9: Confirm your admission by paying the enrollment fee and completing the required formalities.
