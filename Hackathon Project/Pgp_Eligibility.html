Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must meet the following criteria to be eligible for postgraduate courses:

  • Completed a bachelor's degree in a relevant field from a recognized university.
  • Minimum aggregate marks:
    • General Category: 50%
    • SC/ST/OBC: 45%
  • Proficiency in English (both written and spoken).
  • Some courses may require additional qualifications or entrance exams (e.g., GATE for M.Tech.).

Additional Information

The fee includes tuition, library access, and examination charges. Hostel and mess fees are not included and will be charged separately for students opting for on-campus accommodation. Scholarships and financial aid options are available for eligible candidates.
