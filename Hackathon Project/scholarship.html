Explore Scholarships for Students

1. National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

The National Scholarship Portal is a one-stop platform for students to access various scholarships offered by the central and state governments as well as other government agencies. Key schemes under NSP include:

  • Pre-Matric Scholarship: For students from economically weaker sections studying in Classes 1 to 10.
  • Post-Matric Scholarship: For students from minority communities pursuing higher education after Class 10.
  • Merit-cum-Means Scholarship: For meritorious students from minority communities enrolled in professional and technical courses.
Apply Now

2. Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students

This scheme provides financial assistance to academically outstanding students from low-income families to support their higher education in colleges and universities. Key features include:

  • Applicable for students who scored above 80% in their Class 12 board exams.
  • Offers an annual scholarship amount for undergraduate and postgraduate studies.
  • Ensures the continuity of support for the entire duration of the course, subject to academic performance.
Apply Now

3. Post-Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Castes (SC) Students

This program aims to assist SC students in pursuing post-matriculation or post-secondary education. Highlights include:

  • Coverage of tuition fees, maintenance allowance, and other related costs.
  • Support for students studying in recognized institutions across India.
  • Targeted towards students with an annual family income below a specified threshold.
Apply Now

4. Pragati Scholarship for Girls

The Pragati Scholarship is designed to empower women by encouraging them to pursue higher education in technical fields. Key details include:

  • Available for up to two girl children per family.
  • Scholarship includes tuition fee waiver and a monthly allowance.
  • Eligibility requires enrollment in AICTE-approved technical institutions.
Apply Now

5. Saksham Scholarship for Differently-Abled Students

This scholarship supports differently-abled students pursuing technical education. Features include:

  • Assistance for tuition fees and incidental expenses.
  • Open to students with more than 40% disability certified by a competent authority.
  • Applicable for courses in AICTE-approved institutions.
Apply Now

6. Ishan Uday Scholarship for North Eastern Region

To promote higher education and reduce the dropout rate in the North-Eastern region, this scholarship offers:

  • Monthly financial support for undergraduate and postgraduate courses.
  • Eligibility includes domicile in one of the North-Eastern states and enrollment in a recognized institution.
  • Preference for students from families with lower income levels.
Apply Now
